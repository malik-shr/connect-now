# 5-Minute Hackathon Pitch & User Stories Guide

This guide outlines a seamless 5-minute presentation script you can run live during the hackathon, using the exact features and **Persona Switcher** integrated into the connect-now mockup workspace.

---

## ⏱️ Presentation Timing (5 Minutes)
* **0:00 - 1:00** | **The Problem Pitch**: Present the 3 Grid Connection bottlenecks (Data Ping-Pong, Status Silos, Role Gaps).
* **1:00 - 2:00** | **Story 1: The Customer**: Solves *Status Silos*.
* **2:00 - 3:30** | **Story 2: The Installer**: Solves *Role Gaps* and *Data Ping-Pong* in real time.
* **3:30 - 4:30** | **Story 3: The VNB Grid Operator**: Solves *Instant Digital Approvals*.
* **4:30 - 5:00** | **Summary**: Key Business Metrics (Connection time reduced from 6 months to 6 days).

---

## 👥 User Stories & Demo Scripts

### 1. The Customer (Asset Operator)
> **"As a Customer, I want to track the status of my solar application in real time so I'm never left in the dark."**

* **Demo Steps**:
  1. Open the login screen (`/login`). Click the **Customer 1 (user1@connect-now.io)** card.
  2. Explain the **Data Isolation**: Customer 1 only sees their own two active PV projects.
  3. Click on **PV Rooftop Installation (North)** and navigate into the **Status Portal**.
  4. Point to the progress bar and show that the **Operator Power of Attorney** is missing.
  5. Go back and click **"Request Support"** to start collaboration.
* **Key Talking Points**: 
  * *"Grid connections usually feel like a black box. In connect-now, the customer has a live, interactive timeline showing exactly who has the ball: Customer, Installer, or Grid Operator."*
  * *"Seeing a document is missing, the customer loops in their certified installer via a shared workspace chat."*

---

### 2. The Installer (Certified Partner)
> **"As an Installer, I want to co-author the technical documents and assist the customer in real time to prevent invalid data submissions."**

* **Demo Steps**:
  1. Log out, go to `/login`, and select **Max Weber (install@connect-now.io)**.
  2. Show that Max only sees projects assigned to him. Open the support chat.
  3. Explain the **Role-Aware Chat**: Max sees himself on the right ("Me") and Customer 1 on the left.
  4. Click the demo helper **"Simulate Customer Upload"**. Watch the customer's signed PDF arrive in chat.
  5. Explain how the project status automatically upgrades to **"In Review"**.
* **Key Talking Points**:
  * *"Installers have the technical data; customers have the signatures. connect-now bridges this gap with a shared chat workspace."*
  * *"We simulate the customer uploading the signed document. The installer instantly validates it, submits the package, and the status updates across the grid database in real time."*

---

### 3. The Grid Operator (Verteilnetzbetreiber / VNB)
> **"As a Grid Operator, I want a clean dashboard of validated digital connection requests so I can issue grid access approvals in one click."**

* **Demo Steps**:
  1. Log out, go to `/login`, and select **Dr. Andrea Kraft (admin@connect-now.io)**.
  2. Show the **VNB Portal Dashboard** (Connected Capacity charts, success rates, processing times).
  3. Go to **Connection Requests**, select `PV Rooftop Installation (North)`. Notice the documents (like the Power of Attorney) are checked green.
  4. Toggle the status from **In Review** to **Approved**.
* **Key Talking Points**:
  * *"Grid operators are overwhelmed by paper. By matching the official VDE Data Set 3.0 standard and pre-validating submissions, connect-now acts as a digital buffer."*
  * *"Andrea Kraft checks the pre-validated checklist, sees the green flags, and issues the official approval in one click. The customer's status timeline turns green instantly."*

---

## 🚀 The Value Proposition
* **No More Data Ping-Pong**: The installer checks and co-signs documents before submission.
* **100% Transparency**: The live timeline keeps all stakeholders aligned.
* **Time to Grid Connection**: Slashed from **6 months** (paperwork/emails) to **6 days** (digital straight-through processing).
